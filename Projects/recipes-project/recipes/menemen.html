Menemen

Menemen is a food that was forged in the heart of the Ottoman empire. At a time were the Ottomans ruled the better part of the known world, a hearty menemen for breakfast is what fueled their everyday endeavours.

Menemen is a breakfast dish that comprises of eggs, tomatoe, onions, peppers and all types of other good shit. The meal is jampacked with natural sources of protein like eggs, and adding a high protein cheese to top it off makes it even better.

Eat menemen. Stay based.

Ingredients
  • Eggs that your chickens layed this morning
  • Cheese made from the milk of the cattle you just slaughtered
  • Onions and peppers
  • Tomatoes from your farm
